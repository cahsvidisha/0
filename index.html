about us

Central Academy is a trusted name in education in Vidisha. Established in 1997, we offer quality education from Nursery to Class 12 under the MP Board curriculum. Our mission is to provide a safe, supportive, and disciplined environment where children learn with joy and grow with confidence. We focus on strong academics, moral values, and individual attention to every child.
